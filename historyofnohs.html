History of Negros Occidental High School

Our Beginnings

Negros Occidental High School (NOHS), founded in 1902 as Instituto Rizal, has established a long-standing "Culture of Excellence" as the largest secondary educational institution in the province.

Growth and Development

Over the decades, NOHS expanded its facilities, introduced new academic programs, and strengthened its commitment to excellence. The school continuously adapts to modern educational standards while preserving its proud traditions.

NOHS Today

Today, NOHS continues to nurture responsible, intelligent, and capable students ready to contribute positively to society. It remains a symbol of academic excellence in Negros Occidental.
